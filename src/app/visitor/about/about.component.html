Presentation of the ICT department

Since its creation in 2000, the ICT Department of ENIT has sought to meet the needs of the Tunisian ecosystem for quality engineers in the fields of Information and Communication Technologies through the establishment of two engineering courses: Telecommunications Engineers and Computer Engineers.
  • Computer Engineer

    • The objective of the ENIT's Computer Science programme is to train engineers capable of applying solid scientific, technical and professional skills in the field of computer science in particular, and Information and Communication Technologies in general, in order to support the needs of organisations and companies in the management of their information systems through the design, development, implementation and supervision of various hardware and software architectures.

  • Telecommunications Engineer

    • The ENIT Telecommunications Engineer is required to analyse, design, develop, experiment and produce Communications Systems intended for diversified applications, while integrating the appropriate computer developments.
